Welcome To MyTech.

What is MyTech?

MyTech is a discord-orientated tech support service ran by volunteers from all over the world. Founded in November of 2019 by Dale Suffolk and Josh Bannister, MyTech has since grown to an 800+ strong community.

What do we do?

Here at MyTech we offer services such as virus/malware removal, hardware installation guides, general troubleshooting, and custom computer build guidance.

How much do these services cost?

Absolutely nothing. All of our staff are volunteers and we believe you shouldn't have to spend a fortune to get your computer back up and running.
